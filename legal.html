Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: January 2026 · Maango Agri-Tech Pvt. Ltd., Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India

🔒 Maango is committed to your privacy and data security. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect all user data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide secure marketplace services:

  • Registration data: Name, email, mobile number, city/district
  • KYC documents: Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, or Driving License numbers
  • Business/farm details provided voluntarily
  • Transaction and communication metadata
  • Usage data to improve platform experience

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Identity verification and fraud prevention
  • Connecting buyers with verified farmers and experts
  • Enabling encrypted peer-to-peer communication
  • Sending service notifications and security alerts
  • Improving AI-powered farming recommendations

3. KYC Document Security

All KYC documents uploaded on Maango are:

  • Transmitted via TLS 1.3 encrypted connections
  • Stored in access-controlled cloud storage buckets
  • Never shared publicly or sold to third parties
  • Accessible only by authorized Maango compliance staff
  • Subject to deletion on verified account closure request

Submitting fake or fraudulent documents will result in immediate permanent account suspension and may be reported to relevant authorities.

4. Escrow & Smart Contracts

Contracts drafted on this platform represent binding digital agreements between registered parties. The escrow system ensures:

  • Funds or milestones remain locked until verified by a registered Expert
  • All contract metadata is stored immutably in our secure database
  • Disputes are mediated by our verified Expert network

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as required by Indian law (typically 7 years for financial records). You may request data deletion by contacting us at privacy@maango.in.

6. User Responsibilities

By using Maango, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Not use the platform for fraudulent or illegal activities
  • Maintain the confidentiality of your account credentials
  • Report suspicious activity to support@maango.in

7. Third-Party Services

Maango uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication and real-time database
  • Supabase — PostgreSQL database and secure file storage
  • Google Gemini AI — Agricultural knowledge and AI chat features
  • Google Fonts — Typography rendering

For any privacy concerns, data requests, or legal inquiries:
